Features

Designed with intention

Every feature exists for a reason. Nothing more, nothing less.

Instant Wake

Your screen lights up the moment a notification arrives. Never miss what matters while your phone sits on the desk.

Pocket Mode

Intelligently detects when your phone is in a pocket or bag, and stays off. Saves battery where it counts.

App Filtering

Choose exactly which apps can wake your screen. Full control over what deserves your attention.

Dark Mode

A beautiful dark interface that feels at home on any AMOLED display. Easy on the eyes, easy on the battery.

No Internet

Runs entirely on your device. Zero data collected, zero servers contacted. Your privacy is absolute.

Lightweight

Minimal footprint, negligible battery impact. Built in Kotlin for native performance that just works.

How it works

Three steps to never miss a thing

Set it once and forget it. WakeUpScreen handles the rest.

1

Install and grant permission

WakeUpScreen only needs notification access to listen for incoming alerts. No other permissions required. Your data never leaves your device.

2

Choose your apps

Select which apps should wake your screen. Let important messages through while keeping the noise out. Adjust anytime.

3

That's it, live your life

WakeUpScreen runs silently in the background. When a notification comes in, your screen lights up. When your phone's in your pocket, it stays dark. Simple as that.
